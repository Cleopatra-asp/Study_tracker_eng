📚Study Tracker
A beautifully designed, offline-first study tracking web app — built for students who want to take control of their learning.


✨ What It Does<br>
Study Tracker helps students log daily study sessions, track subjects, and visualize progress — all without needing an account or internet connection.<br>
📖 Log study sessions by subject and duration<br>
📊 Visualize progress with charts and summaries<br>
🎯 Set daily goals and track streaks<br>
💾 Offline-first — works without internet (PWA)<br>
🌙 Dark & Light mode support<br>
📱 Mobile-first responsive design<br>
🚀 Live Demo<br>
🔗 Try it here →<br>
🛠️ Built With<br>
HTML5 — Single-file architecture<br>
CSS3 — Premium UI, mobile-first<br>
Vanilla JavaScript — No frameworks, no dependencies<br>
localStorage — Data persistence<br>
PWA — Installable on any device
<br>
💡 Why I Built This
<br>
Students waste hours figuring out what to study next. This tool gives them clarity — a simple, beautiful log that shows exactly where their time is going.<br>
🔧 How to Use<br>
Open index.html in any browser — or visit the live demo<br>
Add your subjects<br>
Log your study sessions daily
Watch your progress grow 📈
